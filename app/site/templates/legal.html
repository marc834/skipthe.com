Attribution

skipthe.com aggregates links to publicly available news items. Every item on this site links back to its original publisher. We do not republish article bodies — only the headline (as written by the source), a short neutral summary written by an AI moderator, and metadata identifying the source.

Take-down requests

If you are the original publisher of an item, or a person named in one, and you would like an item removed from this site, please use the contact form. We aim to respond within 48 hours.

No warranty

Items are surfaced by an automated AI moderator. We do not guarantee accuracy, completeness, or timeliness. Always verify with the original source before acting on anything you read here. Items labeled unverified come from sources we cannot independently confirm.

Privacy

We do not require accounts. We do not run analytics or tracking scripts. Server access logs (standard nginx logs) are kept for operational purposes and rotated regularly. The contact form sends your message to the site operator and is processed by a third-party form-to-email provider; review their privacy notice if you submit personal information.
